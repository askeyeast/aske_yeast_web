The ASKE YEAST GROUP develops mathematical and experimental models of yeast colony growth. Our interdisciplinary approach deepens understanding of fundamental growth mechanisms and their implications for biofilm-forming yeasts of medical and technological importance. We offer PhD scholarships in mathematical & statistical modelling, image analysis, data science and microbiology.
